How does it work?

  1. The game will pick a 4-color code. Some colors may be repeated.
  2. You have 10 guesses to find the code: You must guess the correct colors for each location.
  3. To guess, fill in all 4 spaces on the first row and click 'checkâ€™.
  4. After you guess, four tiny dots will appear beside the row.
    • Each orange dot means that one guessed color is correct, but is in the wrong space.
    • Each green dot means one color is in the right space.
    • The order of the dots means nothing.
  5. Use the feedback from your previous try to guess again
  6. Continue until you win or there are no more guesses left.

Good Luck!
